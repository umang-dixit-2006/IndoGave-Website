THE INDOGAVE PROCESS

01

Agave Harvest

We hand-select 8-10 year old Blue Weber agave at peak sweetness, sustainably harvested from Indian soil.

02

Slow Roasting

Traditional brick ovens roast the agave for 72 hours to develop complex caramel notes without bitterness.

03

Natural Fermentation

Wild yeast fermentation over 7 days creates our signature smooth profile - no additives or accelerants.

04

Double Distillation

Copper pot distillation removes impurities while preserving the agave's essential character.

05

American Oak Aging

Select expressions rest in charred Indian oak barrels for nuanced vanilla and spice notes.

No additives. No shortcuts. Just pure, uncompromising Indian agave spirit.
